Connect with Trusted Owner Effortlessly

Finding the right job for your driving skills shouldn't be a hassle. DRIVIO simplifies the process by connecting you with vetted, trustworthy vehicle owners in just a few clicks. Our user-friendly platform allows you to create a driver profile, browse available job listings, and establish connections quickly and securely. Experience the convenience of DRIVIO and ensure you're always in demand with reliable job opportunities.

Our Services and Features

Drivo provides drivers with easy job browsing and application capabilities, while allowing vehicle owners to efficiently post job opportunities, search for qualified candidates, and manage communications. The platform ensures secure transactions and fosters trust through its rating and review system, enhancing reliability in the driving community.


Real Time Notification

On Drivo, when a vehicle owner picks a driver for a job, the app quickly notifies the driver with all the details: where and when to start, and what needs to be done. This helps drivers be ready and respond fast, making everything smoother and customers happier.


Job Matching

Drivo app efficiently connects drivers with suitable job opportunities based on location, availability, and vehicle type. Matching drivers with suitable jobs helps everyone find the right fit quickly. This makes the process smoother and saves time.


Owner Feedback

Drivers on Drivo receive ratings and feedback from car owners. This ongoing process fosters improvement, boosting trust across the user base. It helps drivers continuously refine their skills and consistently meet customer expectations effectively.


Payment Process

This system ensures drivers get paid promptly and securely through online and offline transactions, fostering trust and efficiency. Owners are reminded to pay promptly. This process helps maintain a reliable and transparent payment system.


Performance

Drivers on Drivo can track their performance metrics, enabling them to monitor efficiency trends. This feature helps drivers proactively monitor their progress, make informed decisions to enhance performance, maximize their earnings, and strive for continuous improvement.


In App Support

In-App Support on Drivo is dedicated to assisting drivers and owners with platform-related queries and issues promptly. Whether it's technical problems, troubleshooting, or many other details, this service boosts driver and owner satisfaction by ensuring smooth operations.
